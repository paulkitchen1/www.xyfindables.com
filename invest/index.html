XY Findables™ is currently offering a Title IV investment to allow investors to buy ownership in this early stage startup. Share prices are currently $1 each and requires a minimum investment of only $350. There is currently a limited number of shares available for sale.

The Company

XY-The Findables Company follows a single guiding principle: users should never lose anything important again. Since 2014, we have found continued success in establishing the Findables market with our flagship product the XY Finder™, a Bluetooth LE tracking unit. Now, we’re aiming to break the mold and provide our customers with a technologically diverse and complete line of Findables.

The Opportunity

At XY-The Findables Company, the crowdfunding ethos is in our blood. Our first supporters, customers, and evangelists were the early adopters of Kickstarter. It was their involvement and feedback that helped make the company a success. We think that equity crowdfunding, through Regulation A+, is a natural next step for a company like ours. It gives investors at all income levels a chance to be early adopters and gives our supporters a real stake in our success, one with the potential for tangible long-term rewards.
