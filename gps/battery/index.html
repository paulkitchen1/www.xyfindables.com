XY GPS Battery & Range

The XY GPS is designed to maximize battery life in a number of different situations.
It will automatically switch modes depending on the distance to a connected phone or tablet.
Each setting is described in detail below, along with the aproximate battery life if the device were to remain in that situation.

Standard

  • The XY GPS will report its location at regular intervals.
  • Lasts about 1 day
  • Recommended charging: every day

Bluetooth Assisted

  • If the XY GPS comes in range of a connected phone or tablet, it will automatically switch to Bluetooth mode, saving battery.
  • When it leaves Bluetooth range, it will automatically switch back to GPS mode.
  • Works best for items that are near home.
  • Lasts between a week to a month (depending on time spent in Bluetooth range)
  • Recommended charging: every 2 weeks
  • Ex: Pets or children

Bluetooth Only

  • If the XY GPS never goes out of range, it will remain in Bluetooth mode and report its location through the connected phone or tablet.
  • Lasts for up to 3 months
  • Recommended charging: every 2 months
  • Ex: Pets or smaller personal items

Battery Pack Extended

  • An external battery pack can be added to greatly increase the battery life of the XY GPS
  • Lasts +1 day for every 500 mAh
  • Connects via micro USB
  • (Battery pack not included)
  • Recommended charging: life of battery pack
  • Ex: Bike or larger personal items

Externally Powered

  • The XY GPS can be connected to an external power supply to power to the device indefinitely
  • Lasts as long as there is power
  • Connects via micro USB
  • Recommended charging: N/A
  • Ex: Car or other large powered items
