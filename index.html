Find Your Items with Any Device

XY Findables makes it possible to find all your things. Use your phone to find your keys, use your keys to find your phone, and use our products to find a little more peace of mind.

Ring Your Things

Use your phone to find anything with an attached Findable device. See their locations, and make them ring directly from the free XY Find It App.

Ring Your Phone

With the press of a button, you can use your XY Devices to find your phone. You can now easily find your lost phone, even when it is on silent!

IoT Ready

XY creates devices with the Internet of Things in mind. With IFTTT and Zapier support, you can use your Findable devices to connect to other smart devices.

Global Lost And Found

All XY Users are a part of the global Lost and Found Community. So even when you are out of range of your devices, the XY community can help you out.

KeepNear Technology

XY devices use KeepNear technology to ensure that you never leave things behind. Get notifications pushed directly to your phone to keep what matters to you.

Replaceable Batteries

XY Devices are designed for maximum life. Using replaceable and rechargable batteries, you don't have to throw out your device when the battery is dead.
