The Findables Market

"Our goal is to be the leader in the Findables space by defining it, producing the best quality products, and providing the best user and owner experiences." -Arie Trouw, CEO

At XY Findables, the crowdfunding ethos is in our blood. Our first supporters, customers, and evangelists were the early adopters of Kickstarter. It was their involvement and feedback that helped make the company a success. We think that equity crowdfunding, through Regulation A+, is a natural next step for a company like ours. It gives investors at all income levels a chance to be early adopters and gives our supporters a real stake in our success, one with the potential for tangible long-term rewards.

As Owner/Users, investors give us the support and feedback we need to define the Findables market and they share in our success.

In 2014, we experienced great success on Kickstarter. Our campaign pre-orders for the first generation of XY Finders surpassed $200,000, putting us in the top 1% of all Kickstarter projects. Following the campaign, we quickly accelerated our retail presence, both online and with third party retailers, and established distributor and branding partnerships.

Rather than being an exclusively Bluetooth Finder, GPS, QR sticker, or app company that uses different technologies and form-factors, we strive to build a complete Findables ecosystem, create product unity and define a market with overwhelming potential.

That potential is found in three burgeoning markets: wearables - technology you wear, nearables - “smart” technology that reacts to the environment, and Findables - technology that lets you monitor the whereabouts of things that matter to you.

In 2015, the wearables market was worth over $15 billion, whereas the nearables market stood just above the $200 million mark. By 2019, CCS Insight estimates that the wearables market will explode, growing to over $25 billion, while the nearables market is expected to surpass $4 billion over the same period.
