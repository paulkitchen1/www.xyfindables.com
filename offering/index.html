SEC Filing and Documentation

The SEC (Securities and Exchange Commission) is the government agency that regulates the stock market and protects investors to make sure their money is safe and in the right place. They're the same agency that approved Google, Facebook, Tesla, Snapchat, and all the biggest companies in the US to go public and sell their stock.

Out of all the startups nationwide, XY Findables was selected for an exemption by the SEC and was granted Regulation A approval. Typically, you have to be a millionaire to invest in startups. It's actually illegal for anyone who isn't a millionaire to invest in these deals that can change your life forever.
